Site Plan — Liahona Solutions

Branding and content plan for a small Archer GRC consulting firm.

Branding

Site Name & Logo

Site Name: Liahona Solutions

Why this name? The “Liahona” symbolizes guidance and direction. We help organizations navigate complex Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) work in Archer—so this name captures the heart of our value.

Logo (concept): Minimal compass/Liahona sphere + wordmark “Liahona Solutions.”

Purpose

Site Purpose

This site presents Liahona Solutions as a trustworthy partner for Archer GRC implementation, governance, and optimization. It outlines core services (QuickStart, Enterprise Issues Management, Change Control, Integrations, Dashboards, Training), showcases outcomes and credibility, and channels visitors toward a free assessment or a helpful Archer Health Checklist download.

Content Drivers

Scenarios (Visitor Questions)

  • “How quickly can you stand up a governed Enterprise Issues Management workflow in Archer?”
  • “Can you integrate Archer with ServiceNow/Jira and keep data quality high?”
  • “What does your Change Control & Release governance process look like for reducing production defects?”
  • “What's included in a free Archer Health Assessment, and what happens after?”
Visual System

Color Schema

A church-inspired palette for clarity and trust. These colors are also used in this document:

  • Primary Navy (#003366): headings, accents, footer background.
  • Emerald Accent (#0D9488): links, buttons, highlights.
  • Ink (#0f172a): body text.
  • Background (#F9FAFB): page background.
  • Card (#ffffff): section cards.

Usage

  • Headings & UI Accents: Primary Navy
  • CTAs/Links/Highlights: Emerald Accent
  • Body Text: Ink
  • Backgrounds: Light Gray/White (BG & Card)
Typography

Typography

  • Inter — Headings (H1–H3). Clear, modern, and highly legible for titles and section headers.
  • Noto Sans — Body copy. Comfortable reading on all devices, neutral tone.
